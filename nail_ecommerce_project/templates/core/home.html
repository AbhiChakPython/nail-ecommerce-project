How to Choose the Right Nail Extension for Your Lifestyle?

When it comes to choosing the perfect nail extension in Pune, consider your lifestyle. Active routines like sports or manual labor benefit from durable options such as acrylic or gel.

If your lifestyle is more laid-back, try silk or fiberglass for a lighter, natural look. Some options require salon visits every 2–3 weeks, while others like dip powder or press-ons are low maintenance.

Unlocking the Secrets of Nail Extension
